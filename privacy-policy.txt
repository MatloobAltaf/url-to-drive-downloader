Privacy Policy of URL to Drive Downloader


Last Updated: April 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how URL to Drive Downloader ("we",
 "our", or "app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect
When you use URL to Drive Downloader, we collect the following information:
- URLs you provide for downloading content
- File names you specify for downloaded content
- Google Drive folder IDs you select for file storage
- Basic user account information necessary for Google Drive access

How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect solely for the purpose of:
- Downloading content from the URLs you provide
- Saving downloaded content to your Google Drive
- Organizing content in your specified Google Drive folders
- Providing you with information about downloaded files

Google API Services
URL to Drive Downloader uses Google API Services to access your Google Drive. Our use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy (https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage and Retention
URL to Drive Downloader does not persistently store your data outside of your Google Drive. We do not maintain databases of your downloaded files or URLs. Information is processed only at the time of download and is not retained after the operation is complete.

Data Sharing
We do not share your personal information or download history with third parties. The only data transfers that occur are:
- Between our app and the URL you specify for content download
- Between our app and your Google Drive to save downloaded content

Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we process about you
- Correct inaccuracies in your personal information
- Delete your personal information
- Restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
- Revoke permission for our app to access your Google Drive

Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
